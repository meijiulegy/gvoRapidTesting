Instructions:

Do not return to previous pages during the test.

Do not resize the windows.

Each visual field test takes less than 1 minute. You may take a break between tests. The entire process should take 5-10 minutes without breaks.

You will start with a practice round of visual field test, followed by four rounds of distance calibration, each followed by an additional round of visual field test.

For the distance calibrations, you will need: an A4 sized paper/notebook, and a rope to measure distance with.

During a visual field test:

  • Dim the lighting in the environment.
  • Put on your corrective lenses or reading glasses.
  • Keep your head still.
  • Cover the eye that is not being tested, fixate the other eye on the yellow target at the center of the screen at all times.
  • Press spacebar when you see a stimulus.

Results will be displayed after each visual field test.

After the later rounds of test, you will also be asked to assign a score to the distance calibraion method based on ease of use.
